Why Dr Kanwar ?

 

Dr. AJ Kanwar is a renowned dermatologists of national and international fame. He graduated (MBBS) from All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIMS, New Delhi) in 1969. He has over 45 years of expertise in Vitiligo, Psoraisis, Eczemas, Pemphigus and a specialist in skin disorders amongst children. Dr AJ Kanwar is one of the most experienced dermatologist in India. An MBBS and MD from AIIMS, Dr Kanwar has a taught at PGI Chandigarh. He has over 560 publications to his credit and his work is quoted extensively in all major text books of Dermatology. First in India to report efficacy of rituximab in pemphigus, he has treated about 150 patients with rituximab.


 

Author of more than 560 publications in various national and international journals

 

STD Specialist and Leprologist, Invited speaker at countless International conferences, Past Vice President and life member of IADVL, Organizing Secretary of Dermacon - 2008, Co-author of Textbook of Surgical Management of Vitiligo published by Blackwell, Member of Pediatric Society of Dermatology, USA, On the editorial board of Pediatric Dermatology and ANNALS of Dermatology, South Korea, Reviewer for clinical and experimental dermatology, British journal of dermatology and pediatric dermatology.
