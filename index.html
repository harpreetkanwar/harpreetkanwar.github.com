Why Dr AJ Kanwar ?



Dr. AJ Kanwar is a renowned dermatologist (skin specialist) of national and international fame.
He graduated (MBBS) from All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIMS, New Delhi) in 1969.
He's an expert in diseases like Vitiligo, Psoriasis, Pemphigus, Acne, Children Skin Disorders and other common problems.


Dr AJ Kanwar is one of the most experienced dermatologist in India. An MBBS and MD from AIIMS New Delhi, Dr Kanwar has a taught at PGI Chandigarh as Head of Department of Dermatology, Venerology and Leprology.

First in India to report efficacy of rituximab in pemphigus, he has treated about 150 patients with rituximab.


Patient profiles include patients from all over the globe.

According to Dr AJ Kanwar the satisfaction which one gets after seeing his patients completely cured can not be described in words.
He distinctly remembers one of his pemphigus patients seen by him in 1988 who had not eaten 'roti' for 6 months because of pemphigus lesions in mouth.
Now the patient is cured and disease free since 23 years.

Dr AJ Kanwar has had extensive training at St Johns Institute of Dermatology, London in 1995 and more recently at Kurume, Japan in 2010.

At the clinic you will find services such as Hairfall Treatment, Facial Peels, Botox, Fillers, PRP, Biologics, Laser Hair Reduction, Dark Circles / Blemishes / White Patches Removal, Treatment of Sexually Acquired Infections and other related diseases.
