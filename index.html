Why Dr Kanwar ?

 

Dr. AJ Kanwar is a renowned dermatologists of national and international fame. He graduated (MBBS) from All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIMS, New Delhi) in 1969.
He has over 45 years of expertise in Vitiligo, Psoraisis, Eczemas, Pemphigus, Acne and a specialist in skin disorders amongst children.

Dr AJ Kanwar is one of the most experienced dermatologist in India. An MBBS and MD from AIIMS, Dr Kanwar has a taught at PGI Chandigarh.

He has over 560 publications to his credit and his work is quoted extensively in all major text books of Dermatology.

First in India to report efficacy of rituximab in pemphigus, he has treated about 150 patients with rituximab.

Patient profiles include patients from all over the globe. He has also treated a lot of children suffering from pemphigus.
His two publications of pemphigus in children are most extensively cited.

 

According to Dr Kanwar the satisfaction which one gets after seeing his patients completely cured can not be described in words. He distinctly remembers one of his pemphigus patients seen by him in 1988 who had not eaten 'roti' for 6 months because of pemphigus lesions in mouth.
Now the patient is cured and disease free since 23 years.
Dr Kanwar has had extensive training in Pemphigus at St Johns Institute of Dermatology, London in 1995. And more recently at Kurume, Japan in 2010.
Pemphigus is a potentially fatal disease It is an autoimmune disorder in which the body starts forming antibodies against skin. And mucous membranes resulting in blisters. It is important to diagnose it and treat early.
